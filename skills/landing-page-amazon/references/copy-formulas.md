# Landing Page Amazon - Copy Formulas

## Headline公式

### 基础公式
```
[Emotion] + [Specific Result] + [Timeframe]
```

| 类型 | 公式 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 评测型 | `[Product] Review: [One-sentence verdict] + [Updated Date]` | "Apple Watch Series 11 Review: The Best All-Around Watch in 2026 (Updated March)" |
| 对比型 | `[Product A] vs [Product B]: Which One Wins in [Category]?` | "Apple Watch Series 11 vs Series 10: Which One Should You Buy?" |
| 问题型 | `[Question about pain point]? Here's the Answer.` | "Is the Apple Watch Series 11 Worth Upgrading? Here's the Truth." |

| 价值型 | `[Product] at $[Price]: Is It Worth It?` | "Apple Watch Series 11 at $299: Is It Worth It?" |

## Pros & Cons文案技巧

### Pros（优点）写法
- ✅ 具体化 + 带数据: "40% larger display than Series 6"
- ✅ 场景化: "Read notifications at the beach without raising your wrist"
- ✅ 对比化: "Charges 2x faster than Series 9"

### Cons（缺点）写法
**原则**: 写"无伤大雅"的缺点，展示诚实但不影响购买决策

| ❌ 避免 | ✅ 改为 |
|--------|--------|
| "续航太差只有18小时" | "Battery life is good but not groundbreaking — most users get 1.5 days" |
| "充电线太短" | "Still comes with that short charging cable — you might want a longer one" |
| "贵" | "Not the cheapest option — but the health features justify the price for most" |
| "钛金属版本太重" | "Titanium models add durability but are noticeably heavier than aluminum" |

## FAQ文案技巧

### 从People Also Ask提取问题
在Google搜索产品名，找到"People Also Ask"框中的问题

### 回答公式
```
[Direct answer] + [Brief explanation if needed]
Keep it short: 2-3 sentences max
```

### FAQ示例
```
Q: Is Apple Watch Series 11 worth upgrading from Series 9?
A: Only if you want the new temperature sensor and slightly larger display. 
   Series 9 users with good battery life can comfortably wait another year.

Q: How long does the battery last?
A: Up to 18 hours normally, or 36 hours in Low Power Mode. 
   Most users find it lasts 1.5-2 days with moderate use.

Q: Can I swim with it?
A: Yes — 50m water resistance makes it perfect for swimming, 
   surfing, or any water activity. Just enable Water Lock first.
```

## Meta Description公式

### 公式
```
[Product] + [Key benefit/verdict] + [Target audience] + [CTA] — [Updated date]
```

### 示例
```
Apple Watch Series 11 Review: The most advanced Apple Watch yet with 
larger display, faster charging, and new health sensors. Find out if 
you should upgrade. Updated March 2026.

Best Apple Watch for Most People in 2026: Series 11 delivers the 
best value with all key features at $299. Our honest review covers 
pros, cons, and deals. Updated March 2026.
```

### 字符数
- 理想: 150-160字符
- 包含关键词
- 包含CTA暗示（"find out", "see", "check"）
- 包含日期体现时效性

## Affiliate Disclosure文案

### 标准格式
```
As an Amazon Associate, [site name] earns from qualifying purchases. 
This means we may receive a small commission when you click our 
links and make a purchase on Amazon. This comes at no additional 
cost to you and helps support our editorial process.
```

### 简化格式（页面底部）
```
As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases.
```
